# Member Portal

The Member Portal is used for tracking member information and event management.

- **Repository:** [https://github.com/IEEE-TAMU/portal](https://github.com/IEEE-TAMU/portal)

### Features
- Tracking member information
- Event creation and management
- iCal feed for the Discord bot and Homepage
- Uploading resumes to Cloudflare R2
- Tracking payment status via webhooks from the [Marketplace Handler](../cloudflare/marketplace-handler.md)
