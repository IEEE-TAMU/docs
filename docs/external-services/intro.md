# External Services Overview

This section documents the third-party services and platforms that IEEE TAMU integrates with for operations.

## Communication & Collaboration

- **[Discord](./discord.md)**: Primary communication platform for members and officers.
- **[GroupMe](./groupme.md)**: Secondary messaging platform bridged to Discord.

## Payments & Finance

- **[Flywire](./flywire.md)**: TAMU SOFC payment processing for membership dues.

## Email & Marketing

- **[Brevo](./brevo.md)**: Transactional email provider for automated notifications.

## Cloud & Infrastructure

- **[Cloudflare](./cloudflare.md)**: CDN, DNS, Workers, and R2 storage.
- **[GitHub](./github.md)**: Source control and CI/CD for all IEEE TAMU projects.

## Scheduling & Calendar

- **[Google Calendar](./google-calendar.md)**: Event calendar integration source.
